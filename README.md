# Tower International

Tower International was a global manufacturer of engineered structural metal components and assemblies for the automotive industry, primarily serving original equipment manufacturers (OEMs). The company supplied body-structure stampings, frame and chassis structures, and complex welded assemblies for cars, crossovers, pickups, and SUVs. Tower International was acquired by Autokiniton Global Group in 2019 and merged with L&W Group to form the second largest Body-in-White and vehicle frame supplier in North America.

**Current Parent Company:** [Autokiniton](https://www.autokiniton.com/)
**Wikipedia:** [Tower International](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tower_International)

## APIs

Tower International is a private automotive metal stamping and structural assembly manufacturer. As a Tier 1 automotive supplier, Tower International does not expose public developer APIs or REST web services. Their operations involve direct EDI and proprietary supply chain integrations with OEM customers rather than public API offerings.
